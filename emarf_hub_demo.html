ABOUT

EMARF is a wood CNC service specialized in DIY furniture (Produced by VUILD.co).
This EMARF Hub application is designed as a hub connecting designers and potential EMARF users who are not able to design(including generating 2D/3D manufacturing data) by themselves. With professional designers' help, users will no longer need to spend hundreds of hours on studying how to materialize the awesome ideas in their brain(making 2D 3D models...etc) before actually making something. While users have a brand new choice to create their DIY furniture, designers are able to make extra income with the power of EMARF platform.
