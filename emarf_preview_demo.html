EMARF Preview Display

The preview display for EMARF furniture DIY service

  • #Product

  • #Web

  • #Service

ABOUT

EMARF is a wood CNC service specialized in DIY furniture (Produced by VUILD.co).
By the use of WebGL(Three.js), this preview display transforms the JSON format data(including XYZ axis coordinates) into visible 3D line segments with different colors. While users can see the actual shapes and boolean effects of what they created, it becomes easier for users to check their works before submitting final manufacturing requests.
