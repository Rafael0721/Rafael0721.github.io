ABOUT

This project aims to enable these small robots to collaborate with each other, much like bees or ants, to accomplish complex tasks as a group. In a short research study on swarm robotics, each robot was built using an Arduino Mini and equipped with various types of sensors. The goal is to create a network of robots capable of working together to perform tasks that would be difficult or impossible for a single robot to achieve. By leveraging their collective capabilities, these swarm robots can tackle challenges in areas such as search and rescue, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation.
