InSight

Stop stretching your neck, start transforming

  • #Product

  • #UI/UX

ABOUT

When people use their laptops for a long time, they are forced to stretch necks due to the angle from which they watch laptops. Current laptops are not able to provide users a comfortable viewpoint, therefore, could hurt users' necks gradually. Insight is a concept laptop designed for preventing users’s necks from being sore with one action, and help set them apart from chronic straining.
